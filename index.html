About

Yinsong Wang is a postdoctoral fellow at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology. He is fortunate to be co-advised by Dr. Yu Ding and Dr. Xiao Liu. Yinsong received his Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from Northeastern University. He was fortunate to be mentored by Dr. Shahin Shahrampour. He also earned an M.S. in Manufacturing Systems Engineering and Management from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University and a B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from Shandong University. His research interests span data science, machine learning, information theory, and statistics.
